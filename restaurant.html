About Us

We opened the doors of "The Restaurant" around the August of 2022 for the visitors to experience the kind of ambience and food they never had before. We curated our menu list carefully by keeping seasonal and local factors in our mind. Moreover, it was necessary to make it affordable for our guests as our main motive behind opening this restaurant was never money making but giving the people around us an experience and memory they can cherish forever.
